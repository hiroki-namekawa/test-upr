Values of Rental

The system where upr rents its own pallets is an approach for taking care of the global environment. We can share limited resources effectively and prevent unnecessary loss. The system also reduces mottainai (wasteful) things such as the pallets storage area when not in use, purchase costs, and management labor costs. upr always possesses a variety and a sufficient quantity of pallets to meet customer needs.

Guidance for Rental

You can rent upr pallets in six easy steps.
We combine and offer our know-how for cost reduction. We make possible the effective use of overall logistics materials.

STEP1 : Consultaion
First, contact our sales department.
We can propose a rental system that is best for you.
STEP2 : Cost estimate
The cost depends on the type of rental item, rental quantity, and period.
Period : The rental period needs to be a minimum of 10 days. If the required rental period is less than 10 days, we still charge the fee for 10 days.
Rental fee : For the rental of pallets or other logistics equipment, we calculate as follows:
(the number of days from when the rental is agreed to when the equipment is returned) x (quantity) x (cost of a equipment per day)
STEP3 : Order
Please confirm the details of your rental order: rental item name, quantity, period, fee, depot to receive the items, and the date
STEP4 : Receiving
The cost depends on the type of rental item, rental quantity, and period.
Period : The rental period needs to be a minimum of 10 days. If the required rental period is less than 10 days, we still charge the fee for 10 days.
Rental fee : For the rental of pallets or other logistics equipment, we calculate as follows:
(the number of days from when the rental is agreed to when the equipment is returned) x (quantity) x (cost of a equipment per day)
STEP5 : Return
Please return the items to a nearby depot.
STEP6 : Payment
Please make payment in accordance with our policy:
All invoices received by the end of the month will be paid in full through a bank transfer by the end of the following month. If you wish other payment arrangements, please consult us.

 

Other
If there is any damage, dirt, or unusual odors on the item, which could cause problems during the next use, we may charge it to customers. In case of missing items, customers will be required to compensate for the full market value of the item.
