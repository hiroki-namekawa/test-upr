What's Carsharing system "Carsherry"

Carsharing is a system where several admitted members share vehicles (equipped with a “Carcherry”) and enjoy the convenience of a vehicle at a lower cost. It is a just brand new featuring of circulation system that makes full use of the asset “vehicle”, saving labor and upkeep cost, while CO2 emissions are reduced. In particular, our system is so convenient because members can reserve and even lock and/or unlock vehicle’s door using their own mobile phones.

Characteristics of UPR carsharing business

For enterprisers who plan to start a carsharing business

Our Features

Vehicles can be reserved from a mobile phone or personal computer.
Our In-car equipment is adaptable for all domestic cars and some imported cars.
Billing program is well made, so it is applicable to various price list.
